Programs

Morning School

8:30am to 12:30pm

Early childhood education provided for children from age 2 to age 6. There will be emphasis on building social emotion to cognitive study from very young age. PowerKids has a track record of strong academics and good character transformation.

After School

12:30pm to 3:00pm

Learning happens beyond academics. We start with lunch, followed by homework coaching with teachers. Then, there are a huge variety of enrichment classes for our children to grow holistically!

Daycare

3:00pm to 6:00pm

An extension from Morning to After School, until 6:00pm to pick up your child.
